## 🎨 Consistent Style Notes

- **Background**: Always dark charcoal/black matte textured surface
- **Lighting**: Dramatic side/chiaroscuro — never flat
- **Steam**: Always include wispy steam for hot items
- **Floating elements**: 2-4 ingredients levitating for depth
- **Color temp**: Warm (3500-4000K feel)
- **No text overlays** on the images
- **No people's faces** in food shots (hands OK)
