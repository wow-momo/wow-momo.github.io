Our Story

From Kathmandu Streets
to Manor, TX

A story of family, flavor, and the dream to share Nepal's most beloved street food with Texas.

Chapter One

It Started With a Memory

Growing up in Nepal, momos weren't just food. They were family gatherings around a steaming bamboo basket. They were the smell of garlic, ginger, and cumin filling the kitchen. They were the sound of laughter and the warmth of home.

When we moved to Texas, we brought our spices, our recipes, and our deep love for momos. But we couldn't find a single place that made them the right way — the real way. Hand-folded. Perfectly spiced. Made with soul.

Chapter Two

If Nobody Else Will, We Will

So we did what any momo-obsessed family would do: we opened our own spot. WoW Mo:Mo was born from a simple conviction — Manor, TX deserves a place where the dumplings are handmade with zero shortcuts.

We spent months perfecting our dough, dialing in our spice blends, and testing every fold technique until every single momo met our family's standard. No machine-stamped dumplings. No generic fillings. Just the same momos we grew up eating — the kind that make you close your eyes and savor every bite.

Chapter Three

More Than Mo:Mos

WoW Mo:Mo isn't just a restaurant — it's a bridge between cultures. We want every guest who walks through our doors to experience the warmth of Nepali hospitality, the boldness of our flavors, and the joy that a perfectly folded momo can bring.

Whether you've been eating momos your whole life or you're about to try your very first one, we're here to make it unforgettable. Welcome to our table. Welcome to the family.
